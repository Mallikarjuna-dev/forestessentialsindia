HAIR CARE

HOME > HAIR CARE

In ancient India, special emphasis was given to ayurvedic hair care since beautiful hair has been prized throughout history. Ancient Indian texts have always
depicted women with luxurious tresses. For these hair treatments, apprentices would collect the ingredients prescribed by an Ayurvedic physician, including leaves of
the Brahmi plant, Jatamansi (or spikenard), Amla, Bhringraj, and Japapatti - that were and still are considered essential for the health of hair.All these herbs and roots were
then soaked in Coconut oil for a day or two until the oil became green. The herb enriched oil was then blended over slow fire for a residual paste, which was then strained
to be applied onto hair every night. Such herbal pastes were warmed and stored in copper vessels - which imparted their own nutritional properties to the oil.
